About This Platform

This platform is dedicated to exploring, documenting, and showcasing species found in the Indian subcontinent. It serves as a digital catalog with scientific, ecological, and conservation details for each species. Built with a vision to promote awareness, research, and conservation initiatives.

Key Features

  • 🔍 Real-time species search
  • 📸 Scientific & local names with photos
  • 📚 Habitat and behavior details
  • 📊 Interactive charts (coming soon!)
  • 📍 Species distribution map (coming soon!)
