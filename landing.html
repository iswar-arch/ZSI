About This Platform

Species Explorer is a digital initiative designed to systematically document and showcase the rich biodiversity of the Indian subcontinent. The platform serves as an interactive repository that provides accurate scientific information, ecological context, and conservation insights for a wide range of native species.

Developed with the goal of fostering awareness and supporting research, this resource is aimed at students, researchers, conservationists, and nature enthusiasts alike.
